We offer a Drupal training course as part of Bulcode's education and mentorship initiatives. The one-month program is led by Drupal Technical Architects with more than ten years of experience in the field of Drupal. Participants are full-time committed and work their way through a comprehensive Drupal agenda. Each part of Drupal development is covered, from Site Building to Drupal Performance and Security. Each participant is expected to complete exercises and real-world project tasks in addition to the lectures they attend on a daily basis.
